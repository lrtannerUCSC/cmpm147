Experiment 2 - Generative Spacescape

Description

I wanted to create generative landscapes, but I wanted the to base it off of images of the Pillars of Creation, a space formation recorded by the James-Webb Space Telescope.

Technical

I used perlin noise to create the moving gas clouds that color the background. I also used perlin noise to create the random bends in the pillars. The size of the pillars and the stars were made using random().

Reflection

I found it confusing understanding how to use the perlin noise for both the gas clouds and the pillars, since they use the noise in different ways. Overall I am very satisfied with the final product.
